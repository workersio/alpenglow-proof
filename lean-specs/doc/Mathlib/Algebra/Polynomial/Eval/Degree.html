    theorem Polynomial.eval_monomial_one_add_sub {S : Type v} [CommRing S] (d : ℕ) (y : S) :
    eval (1 + y) ((monomial d) (↑d + 1)) - eval y ((monomial d) (↑d + 1)) = ∑ x_1 ∈ Finset.range (d + 1), ↑((d + 1).choose x_1) * (↑x_1 * y ^ (x_1 - 1))

    A reformulation of the expansion of (1 + y)^d: $$(d + 1) (1 + y)^d - (d + 1)y^d = \sum_{i = 0}^d {d + 1 \choose i} \cdot i \cdot y^{i - 1}.$$
