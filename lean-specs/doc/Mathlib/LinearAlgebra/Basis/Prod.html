    Basis (ι ⊕ ι') R (M × M')

    Basis.prod maps an ι-indexed basis for M and an ι'-indexed basis for M' to an ι ⊕ ι'-index basis for M × M'. For the specific case of R × R, see also Basis.finTwoProd.
