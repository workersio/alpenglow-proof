    Naturals pairing function #

    This file defines a pairing function for the naturals as follows:

     0  1  4  9 16
 2  3  5 10 17
 6  7  8 11 18
12 13 14 15 19
20 21 22 23 24

    It has the advantage of being monotone in both directions and sending ⟦0, n^2 - 1⟧ to ⟦0, n - 1⟧².
