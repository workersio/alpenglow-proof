    theorem AddSubgroup.zsmul_mem_zmultiples_iff_exists_sub_div {R : Type u_1} [DivisionRing R] [CharZero R] {p r : R} {z : ℤ} (hz : z ≠ 0) :
    z • r ∈ zmultiples p ↔ ∃ (k : Fin z.natAbs), r - ↑k • (p / ↑z) ∈ zmultiples p

    z • r is a multiple of p iff r is k * (p / z) above a multiple of p, where 0 ≤ k < |z|.
