    Results on bases of tensor products #

    In the file we construct a basis for the base change of a module to an algebra, and deduce that Module.Free is stable under base change.

    Main declarations #

    • Algebra.TensorProduct.basis: given a basis of a module M over a commutative semiring R, and an R-algebra A, this provides a basis for A ⊗[R] M over A.
    • Algebra.TensorProduct.instFree: if M is free, then so is A ⊗[R] M.

    TensorProduct R A M ≃ₗ[R] ι →₀ A

    Given an R-algebra A and an R-basis of M, this is an R-linear isomorphism A ⊗[R] M ≃ (ι →₀ A) (which is in fact A-linear).
